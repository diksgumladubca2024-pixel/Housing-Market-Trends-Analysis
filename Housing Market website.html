Housing Market Trends Analysis

An Analysis of Sale Prices and Features Using Tableau

This project analyzes housing market trends and examines how factors such as bedrooms, bathrooms, and construction year influence property prices.

Project Overview

The objective of this Tableau project is to identify patterns in housing prices and understand how different property features affect market value.

Relationship Between Bathrooms and Price

The chart shows a positive relationship between the number of bathrooms and average house prices.

Historical Trends by Year Built

Housing prices vary across construction years, highlighting the impact of location, architecture, and historical significance.

Impact of Bedrooms on Average Price

Properties with more bedrooms generally command higher prices due to increased living space and demand.

Key Insights

  • Bathrooms have a strong influence on property prices.
  • Bedrooms show a positive correlation with house value.
  • Historic properties often maintain premium pricing.
  • Location remains a major factor affecting housing prices.
